# Executive Report: Digitalization of Asset Management & Stock Opname
## Focus: Operational Efficiency, Business Impact, and Governance

---

## 1. Business Justification & Strategic Value

### 1.1 Transformation of the Audit Process
Previously, the Stock Opname process (physical asset audit) was conducted manually using paper or manual data entry. This led to high risks of human error and took days for data reconciliation. 

This digital system transforms that process into an **automated, scan-based workflow**, enabling audits to be conducted much faster with guaranteed accuracy.

### 1.2 Primary Business Impact
- **Audit Speed**: Reduction of physical audit time by up to 60% compared to manual methods.
- **Asset Transparency**: Real-time visibility into asset status (Found vs. Missing) across every location or company.
- **Data Accuracy**: Eliminates manual recording errors; physical data is directly synchronized with the system at the moment of scanning.
- **Enhanced Accountability**: Every verified asset has a clear timestamp and responsible party, facilitating tracking in cases of loss or asset movement.

---

## 2. Cost Efficiency

### 2.1 Reduction of Operational Costs
- **HR Efficiency**: Reduces the need for personnel and overtime hours for annual/monthly audit processes.
- **Prevention of Asset Loss**: Early identification of missing or undetected assets helps the company save on replacement costs that should not occur.
- **Inventory Optimization**: With accurate data, the company can avoid duplicate asset purchases because the status and location of old assets are well-documented.

### 2.2 Future Scalability
The system is designed to support company growth. Adding new asset categories or new corporate entities does not require additional system investment, providing long-term cost efficiency.

---

## 3. Governance & Risk Management

### 3.1 Data Risk Mitigation
- **Information Security**: Access to audit data is granted only to authorized personnel (General Affairs Team), ensuring that the confidentiality of company assets is maintained.
- **Audit Trail**: The system stores a complete history of every check, serving as strong supporting documentation for both internal and external auditors.

### 3.2 Report Integrity
The reports generated by this system are objective and based on physical data, reducing the subjectivity or report manipulation that might occur in manual systems. This strengthens the company's standards of Good Corporate Governance.

---

## 4. Conclusion
The implementation of this digital Stock Opname system and barcode identification is not merely a tool update but a strategic investment to increase productivity, tighten asset oversight, and achieve sustainable cost efficiency within the MRA Group.
